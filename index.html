Host Cities

Brasília

Founded in 1960 as the capital of Brazil and located in the middle of the country,Brasilia may not have a long history but the city is like an open-air museum. The capital was planned and developed by iconic architects Oscar Niemeyer and Lúcio Costa

Goiânia

Close to Brasília, about 200km from the country's capital, is Goiânia. One of the largest cities in the interior of Brazil and the capital of the state of Goiás, Goiânia is a true power of national agricultural production.

Cuiabá

The city is surrounded by the impressive Chapada dos Guimarães nature reserves and has rivers filled with delicious fish, a staple of local cuisine. Local club Cuiabá Esporte Clube won promotion to Serie A in 2021.

Rio de Janeiro

In addition to a lot of samba, beaches and leisure, Rio and its hospitable people also eat, breath, and live football.
